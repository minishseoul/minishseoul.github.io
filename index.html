Minish plays house music rooted in the deep sounds of the '90s. The focus is on bass, groove, and especially on how texture changes over time, letting sounds build, drop away, and shift while the mood stays steady. Alongside these deep foundations, he often brings in faster, more driving textures inspired by the early 2000s West Coast US underground scene, especially sounds from San Francisco. This contrast between weight and speed shapes his selections, adding movement and variation without breaking the flow.
